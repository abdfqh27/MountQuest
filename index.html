Mount Rinjani

Mount Rinjani, a stunning natural wonder on the Indonesian island of Lombok, is a dream destination for nature and adventure lovers. With a height of 3,726 meters above sea level, this mountain is not only a climbing field, but also offers extraordinary beauty.

Mount Semeru

Mount Semeru, with the title "Mahameru," is a symbol of natural splendor in East Java, Indonesia. With an altitude of 3,676 meters above sea level, the mountain offers stunning views and an unforgettable climbing experience.

Mount Slamet

Mount Slamet, standing majestically on the border of Central Java and West Java, Indonesia, is a symbol of charming natural beauty. With an altitude of 3,428 meters above sea level, this mountain offers a mesmerizing climbing experience and stunning panoramas.

Mount Sumbing

Mount Sumbing, towering majestically amidst the mountains of Central Java, Indonesia, is an enchanting gem of nature. With a height of 3,371 meters above sea level, the mountain offers an incredible climbing experience and stunning views.

Mount Ciremai

Mount Ciremai, firmly standing in West Java Province, Indonesia, is a symbol of stunning natural beauty. With a height of 3,078 meters above sea level, this mountain offers an interesting climbing experience and mesmerizing scenery.

Mount Prau

Mount Prau, which places itself majestically among the mountains of Central Java, Indonesia, is an amazing natural destination. With an altitude of 2,565 meters above sea level, the mountain presents an enthralling climbing experience and beautiful scenery.
